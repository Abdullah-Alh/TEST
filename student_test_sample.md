1. What is the capital of France?
   - A) Berlin
   - B) Madrid
   - C) Paris
   - D) Rome
   - **Correct Answer:** C

2. Which planet is known as the Red Planet?
   - A) Earth
   - B) Mars
   - C) Jupiter
   - D) Venus
   - **Correct Answer:** B
